Job Title: Data Analyst

Company: TechCorp Solutions
Location: Remote / San Francisco, CA
Type: Full-time

Job Description:

We are seeking a talented Data Analyst to join our growing analytics team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for analyzing complex datasets, creating insightful reports, and providing data-driven recommendations to stakeholders across the organization.

Key Responsibilities:
- Analyze large datasets using SQL and Python to extract meaningful insights
- Create and maintain interactive dashboards using Tableau and Power BI
- Develop statistical models and perform predictive analytics
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand business requirements
- Present findings and recommendations to senior management
- Clean and preprocess data to ensure accuracy and reliability
- Write and optimize SQL queries for data extraction
- Perform A/B testing and analyze results
- Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and create regular reports

Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or related field
- 2+ years of experience in data analysis or business intelligence
- Proficiency in SQL for data querying and manipulation
- Strong Python programming skills (pandas, numpy, matplotlib)
- Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, or similar)
- Solid understanding of statistical concepts and methods
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical thinking skills
- Strong communication skills to present complex data insights

Preferred Qualifications:
- Master's degree in Data Science, Statistics, or related field
- Experience with machine learning algorithms and libraries (scikit-learn)
- Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
- Experience with Git version control
- Familiarity with ETL processes and data pipelines
- Experience with R programming language
- Knowledge of database design and optimization

Technical Skills Required:
- SQL (Advanced)
- Python (Intermediate to Advanced)
- Excel (Advanced - pivot tables, VLOOKUP, macros)
- Tableau or Power BI
- Statistics and Statistical Analysis
- Data Visualization

Soft Skills:
- Strong analytical and critical thinking
- Attention to detail
- Time management and ability to meet deadlines
- Team collaboration
- Clear written and verbal communication
- Ability to translate technical findings to non-technical stakeholders

What We Offer:
- Competitive salary and benefits package
- Remote work flexibility
- Professional development opportunities
- Collaborative and innovative work environment
